This is the first marker for Dev with Min.

The goal is not only to publish polished essays. I want this place to become a durable record of my developer experience: what I build, what I break, what I learn, and what I change my mind about.

The archive starts with three writing intensities:

- **Light Notes** for quick setup notes, tools, keyboards, and small lessons.
- **Field Logs** for experience-based records from real work, migrations, incidents, experiments, and deployments.
- **Deep Dives** for heavier technical writing and computer science philosophy.

The blog itself will also be part of the record: an Astro static site, deployed from my homelab, wrapped as a Nix flake, and improved as I keep writing.
